Project Summary

This Catuane project focused on building a reliable on-farm drying operation with a practical blend of mechanical and automation systems. The plant was designed to support consistent throughput while allowing remote monitoring for faster fault diagnosis and reduced downtime. Our scope included integrating PLC control, material handling flow, and bulk bagging support so the site team could move from manual intervention to a more predictable and efficient process.
